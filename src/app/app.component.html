About Me

With a background in data science and a diverse experience working for prestigious organizations such as Petronas (Malaysia National Oil and Gas Company) and Orsted (World Leader in Renewable Energy), I have honed my expertise in integrated planning and scheduling optimization. My journey in the field of data science has been both exhilarating and fulfilling.

As part of the integrated planning and scheduling optimization team, I have been involved in several transformative projects utilizing cutting-edge machine learning techniques, customized simulations, and optimization modeling. These projects have enabled us to enhance operational efficiency, reduce costs, and optimize resource allocation for complex processes.

Outside my professional engagements, my passion for continuous learning has led me to delve into personal projects focused on computer vision applications, natural language processing (NLP), and frontend development. My free time is dedicated to exploring new horizons and staying updated with the latest advancements in these areas.

Having accumulated multiple years of experience in the field, I am driven by the desire to solve real-world challenges and drive meaningful change through data-driven insights. As I look ahead, I am excited to continue my journey of exploration and innovation, contributing to the transformative potential of data science in shaping a sustainable and prosperous future.
